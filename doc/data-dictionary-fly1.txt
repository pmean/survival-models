This data is a lightly modified subset of data from the Journal of Statistics Data Archive on survival time of fruit flies and is described in the article

Hanley JA, Shapiro SH (1994) Sexual Activity and the Lifespan of Male Fruitflies: A Dataset That Gets Attention. Journal of Statistics Education 2(1). Available at http://ww2.amstat.org/publications/jse/v2n1/datasets.hanley.html.

This data set is copyrighted, but is reproduced here under the fair use provisions of copyright law.

It is used in ~/survival-models/src/survival-lecture-1.Rmd to illustrate computation of the Kaplan-Meier curve. 

This is a text file with a single column of data and no header. There are 25 rows in this file.

The first (and only) column represents time of death in days.
